Recycling Participation Share (GEKAP) SAP Solution

Recycling Participation Share (GEKAP) In line with the principles of sustainable environment and sustainable development of the Environmental Law No. 2871, specific products and articles are recovered at the sales points for bags, from importers for other products is a tax obligation to be collected from taxpayers for the collection of the contribution fee.

With the Sorgera SAP GEKAP solution, it allows you to accurately and completely report the transactions you carry out as a market/importer for products covered by GEKAP, based on sales, purchasing, and production data, based on the unit share determined by the government annually for the product/product groups.

PACKAGING, TIRE, ACCUMULATOR, BATTERY, MINERAL OIL, VEGETABLE OIL, ELECTRIC, ELECTRONIC GOODS, and PHARMACEUTICALS are included in the category of goods subject to declaration and/or contribution payment within the scope of the recovery contribution share (GEKAP).

Effective start date:
- Bags : 01/01/2019
- Other goods: 01/01/2020
