Therefore, we would like to inform you about your rights regarding the use and protection of your personal data within the scope of the Personal Data Protection Law (Law) numbered 6698 in order to protect your fundamental rights and freedoms.

Scope of the General Law

We are referred to as "Data Supervisor" as Sorgera Software Technologies Inc. in the Law. Under the Law, "Data Processing"; All kinds of operations performed on the data, such as obtaining, recording, storing, updating, classifying, sharing or transferring personal data to third parties permitted by legislation, in whole or in part, by automatic or non-automatic means provided that it is part of any data recording system. does

Legal Reasons for Processing Personal Data

Within the scope of the relationship we will establish for the products and services you will receive from our company; We process your personal data for legal reasons in Article 5 of the Law:
- It is clearly stipulated in the laws regulated in Article 5/2 (a),
- It is necessary to process personal data of the parties to the contract, provided that it is directly related to the contract you have signed with our company regulated in article 5/2 (c) or the fulfillment of this contract.
- It is mandatory for our company to fulfill its legal obligation regulated in Article 5/2 (รง),
- Data processing is mandatory for the creation, use or protection of a right regulated in Article 5/2 (e),
- It is mandatory to process data for the legitimate interests of our company, provided that it does not harm your fundamental rights and freedoms regulated in Article 5/2
- For your private personal data; Having your express consent according to Article 6/2 of the Law.

To record your identity, address and other necessary information in order to identify your information in your transactions,
To transmit important information that we need to share with you over your contact information,

With Whom and For What Purposes Do We Share Your Personal Data?

If necessary, we share your personal data only with persons and organizations permitted by the Law. With these people and organizations, we share your information with the program partner organizations that we receive services from or cooperate with in order to carry out our activities, with your permission.

Where Can We Reach Your Personal Data?

Your personal data can be collected in verbal, written or electronic media through system integrations (such as the Identity Sharing System) shared through the head office, public institutions and organizations.

What Are Your Rights Under the Law?

By contacting our company whenever you want your personal data;

- You can learn whether it is processed, the purpose of processing and whether it is used according to its purpose, and if it has been processed, it can ask for information about it,
- In accordance with the law, you can learn the third parties your information is shared at home and abroad,
- If you think your information is incomplete or incorrectly processed, you can request correction,
- Request deletion or destruction of your information within the framework of the conditions stipulated in article 7 of the Law,
- You may request that third parties to whom your information is transferred be notified of your requests specified in articles (c) and (d) and perform the same actions,
- You can object to the emergence of a result against you due to the analysis of your information with automated systems, or if you think that it has been recorded or used illegally and you have suffered damage for this reason, you can request the removal of the damage.

If your application for these purposes requires an additional cost, you may need to pay the fee in the tariff determined by the Personal Data Protection Board. Your requests in your application will be concluded as soon as possible and within 30 (thirty) days at the latest, depending on the nature of the request.

In order to take advantage of your rights under the law, you can send your applications in writing to your registered electronic mail (REM) address, secure electronic signature, mobile signature or from your e-mail address that you have previously notified to our company and registered in our company's system, and to obtain detailed information You can visit the page.
